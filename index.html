Digital Artist & Hobbyist

I'm passionate about neural engineering, XR interfaces, and AI agents for consumer healthcare, entertainment, and robotics.

I also create visual art under the username EphyEsper or EphyWT. I gravitate toward digital sci-fi and high fantasy landscapes.

> More about me <


You can find me researching visuomotor neuroscience at Columbia University in the MS Biomedical + Neural Engineering program. Recently, I received my BS in Computer Science from NYU, where I was fortunate to be a research assistant at the Immersive Computing Lab, AI4CE Lab, and several other labs. I also served as NYU RoboMaster's lead of Computer Vision.

My short term career goal is to pursue a PhD in my area of interest, and explore entrepreneurial ventures along the way. I am always looking for research internships where I can work with deep learning, modeling human perception/cognition, brain-computer interfaces, and applications of mixed-reality.

Aside from those topics, I will always be eager to learn about clean technology for combating global warming and environmental decline, and personal/wearable/soft robotics for improving our daily quality of life.
